Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 30, 2026

MeCo AI ("the App") is a personal memory companion that helps you save and retrieve personal facts, preferences, and reminders. Your privacy is fundamental to how we built this app.

1. Local-First Architecture

All your memories, messages, and settings are stored locally on your device using IndexedDB (via Dexie.js). We do not operate servers, databases, or cloud storage. Your data never leaves your device except when explicitly sent to AI providers as described below.

2. AI Provider API Calls

When you save a memory or ask a question, the App sends text to the AI provider you have configured (OpenAI or Anthropic) for processing. Specifically:

  • Memory extraction: Your input text is sent to the AI provider to extract structured facts.
  • Question answering: Your question and relevant stored memories are sent to the AI provider to generate an answer.
  • Voice transcription: Audio recordings are sent to OpenAI's transcription API to convert speech to text.
  • Image analysis: Photos you attach are sent to the AI provider to extract memory-worthy details.
  • Embeddings: Text is sent to the AI provider (or processed locally) to generate vector embeddings for semantic search.

These API calls are made directly from your device to the AI provider using your own API key. We never proxy, intercept, or store these requests. The AI provider's own privacy policy and data retention policies apply to these calls:

  • OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic Privacy Policy

3. Data We Collect

None. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on our servers. We do not use analytics, tracking pixels, or third-party SDKs that collect user data.

4. API Keys

Your AI provider API keys are stored locally on your device in IndexedDB. They are never sent to us or any third party other than the respective AI provider during API calls.

5. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Delete individual memories using the Memories tab in the App.
  • Delete all data by uninstalling the App or clearing its data in your device settings.
  • No server-side deletion request is needed because we hold no data.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the effective date at the top of this page.
